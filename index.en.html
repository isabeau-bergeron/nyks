Welcome to NYK'S Bistro Pub

Whether you are looking for a place to eat between meetings or for a lively atmosphere for a happy hour, a pre-theatre dinner in the neighbourhood or a late night drink, we are always happy to serve you!

At the bar, have a beer chosen from an extensive list of 14 local and foreign draughts, share a bottle of wine with friends, or enjoy a glass from our wide selection of scotch, whisky and other spirits.

Take a seat in our restaurant to savour classic dishes (burgers, fish & chips, mussels & fries) that made our reputation or sample one of the chef’s daily creations for which NYK'S Bistro Pub is renowned : market fresh fish, wild game, tartars, as well as homemade pasta…

Either alone or in good company, our friendly staff and unpretentious atmosphere will win you over!
